Food
Firstly we are going to lose one of our main sources of food, fish, More than 3 billion people in the world rely on wild-caught and farmed seafood as a significant source of animal protein. Hundreds of millions of fish are killed every day it’s sad although our subconscious mind still doesn’t realize it and if they still don’t, who knows how the future may be? We are gonna lose fishes as well as $276.5 billion. Yes thats right the fishing industry has grown significantly in recent years. Its worldwide market value has been on the rise, reaching a record high in 2020 with a total value of $276.5 billion. Fishing is not the only way….
Eco-Tourism
Eco-tourism is commonly present in most countries, in short, this means tourism attracted by biodiversity and nature. Many may not realize but when you break down trees and plants you are losing a lot of tourists as well. Research shows that tourists get attracted to nature more than buildings!
S&P Global
Resources from S&P Global shows 85% of the world’s largest companies highly depend on nature across their direct operations. This fact means all major companies use biodiversity for their operations and if we get rid of nature (what is happening right now) all these major companies will go under major and substantial losses. This resource also shows 46% of companies have at least one location in Key Biodiversity Area (KBA).
